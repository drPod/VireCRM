# Frameworks on Cloudflare | Sentry for Cloudflare

You can use the Sentry Cloudflare SDK in combination with Sentry meta-framework SDKs like SvelteKit or Remix to instrument your Cloudflare Pages applications that use these frameworks.

* #### [Astro on Cloudflare](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/javascript/guides/cloudflare/frameworks/astro.md)

  Learn how to instrument your Astro app on Cloudflare Workers and capture your first errors.

* #### [Hono on Cloudflare](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/javascript/guides/cloudflare/frameworks/hono.md)

  Learn how to instrument your Hono app on Cloudflare Workers with Sentry.

* #### [Hydrogen with React Router](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/javascript/guides/cloudflare/frameworks/hydrogen-react-router.md)

  Learn how to instrument your Hydrogen app on Shopify Oxygen and capture your first errors and traces.

* #### [Hydrogen with Remix (Legacy)](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/javascript/guides/cloudflare/frameworks/hydrogen-remix.md)

  Learn how to use the Sentry Remix SDK to instrument your Hydrogen app (versions before 2025.5.0).

* #### [Next.js on Cloudflare](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/javascript/guides/cloudflare/frameworks/nextjs.md)

  Learn how to configure Sentry for your Next.js application deployed on Cloudflare Workers.

* #### [Nuxt on Cloudflare](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/javascript/guides/cloudflare/frameworks/nuxt.md)

  Learn how to instrument your Nuxt app on Cloudflare Workers and Pages and capture your first errors with Sentry.

* #### [Remix on Cloudflare](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/javascript/guides/cloudflare/frameworks/remix.md)

  Learn how to instrument your Remix app on Cloudflare Workers and Pages and capture your first errors with Sentry.

* #### [SvelteKit on Cloudflare](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/javascript/guides/cloudflare/frameworks/sveltekit.md)

  Learn how to instrument your SvelteKit app on Cloudflare Workers and Pages and capture your first errors with Sentry.

* #### [TanStack Start on Cloudflare](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/javascript/guides/cloudflare/frameworks/tanstack-start.md)

  Learn how to instrument your TanStack Start app on Cloudflare Workers and capture your first errors with Sentry.
